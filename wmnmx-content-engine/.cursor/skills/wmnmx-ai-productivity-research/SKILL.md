# WMNMX AI + Productivity Research

## Mission

Find practical AI and productivity signals that help women 40+ save time, organize better, communicate better, sell better, and learn with less friction.

AI must be framed as human amplification, not magic and not replacement.

## Priority topics

Research:

- ChatGPT
- OpenAI updates
- Google AI
- Microsoft Copilot
- Meta AI
- Claude / Anthropic
- AI writing workflows
- prompt examples
- productivity tools
- email writing
- content calendars
- customer response
- automation basics
- no-code / low-code
- personal organization
- business organization

## Preferred sources

Prioritize:

- OpenAI official docs and blog
- Google official AI docs/blog
- Microsoft official docs/blog
- Meta official AI updates
- Anthropic official docs/blog
- official product documentation
- reputable universities
- reputable technology reports

Use secondary sources only if they point to a primary source.

## Output language

Research may happen in English.

Final output must be Spanish, simple, clear, and practical.

Keep digital terms like prompt, chatbot, AI, automation, workflow, no-code, low-code when useful. Explain them in Spanish.

Example:

"Prompt: la instrucción que le das a una IA para que te ayude a escribir, ordenar ideas o resolver una tarea."

## Rejection rules

Reject content if:

- it overpromises AI results
- it frames AI as magic
- it requires advanced technical setup with no beginner path
- it has no practical use for women 40+
- it is a viral tool list with no verification
- it has no official source
- it pushes fear of replacement

## Editorial behavior

Use this framing:

"La IA no piensa por ti. Te ayuda a ordenar ideas, escribir más rápido y probar opciones antes de tomar una decisión."

Always include a 10-minute action when possible.
