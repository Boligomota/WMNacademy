# WMNMX Content Modes

Use the mode that best fits the signal. Do not force every signal into the same three formats.

## 1. Señal verificada

Use when there is a timely update from a trustworthy source.

Structure:

- What happened
- Why it matters for women 40+ in Mexico
- What to do today
- Source
- Conversation question

## 2. Explícamelo fácil

Use when the topic is confusing but useful.

Structure:

- Simple definition
- Everyday example
- What not to confuse it with
- One practical use
- Conversation question

## 3. Reto de 10 minutos

Use when the value is an immediate action.

Structure:

- Today's challenge
- Step-by-step action
- What result to expect
- Safety note if needed
- Comment prompt

## 4. Alerta práctica

Use for safety risks without fear-based framing.

Structure:

- Calm alert
- How to recognize the risk
- What to do / not do
- Where to verify
- Shareable reminder

## 5. Mito vs realidad

Use when the audience may have a misconception.

Structure:

- Mito
- Realidad
- Why the confusion exists
- Practical correction
- Question

## 6. Checklist guardable

Use for evergreen guidance.

Structure:

- Checklist title
- 5 to 7 short checks
- When to use it
- One next step
- Save/share prompt

## 7. Caso aplicado

Use when the signal can be translated into work, business, family, or daily life.

Structure:

- Scenario
- Problem
- Digital action
- Example wording or workflow
- Result

## 8. Pregunta detonadora

Use when conversation is more valuable than instruction.

Structure:

- Context sentence
- Clear question
- Optional examples
- Safe invitation to share

## 9. Mini clase

Use when a topic needs a compact step-by-step explanation.

Structure:

- Lesson title
- What you will learn
- 3 to 5 steps
- Common mistake
- 10-minute practice

## Selection rule

Choose the mode after ranking signals. If the best mode is unclear, prefer:

1. Checklist guardable for evergreen safety or productivity.
2. Reto de 10 minutos for action-oriented topics.
3. Pregunta detonadora when audience feedback is the main input.
4. Señal verificada only when the source is recent and important.
